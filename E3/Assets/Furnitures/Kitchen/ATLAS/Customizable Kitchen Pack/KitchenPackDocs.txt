PACKAGE INFORMATION:
Build the kitchen of your virtual dreams with this modular asset pack.  
Designed specifically with customization in mind, this package includes
models and textures that are flexible enough to create hundreds of different
kitchen layouts.  Dozens of texture assets are included to get you started, 
but you can easily integrate your own textures and shaders to make the 
kitchen your own.

Appliance Models
  - Electric Range Stove
  - Refrigerator
  - Dishwasher

Cabinets and Counters</strong>

  - 3 Drawer Cabinet (2 Variations)
  - 4 Drawer Cabinet
  - Single basin sink
  - Double basin sink
  
Textures
  - 4 Variations for each appliance
  - Over 50 textures to customize cabinets and counters
